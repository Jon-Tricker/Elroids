What is 'Elroids'?

An experiment with a threeJS based cross between 'Asteroids' and 'Elite'.

Any comments gratefully recieved. There is a facebook discussion group here.

Controls

Based on 'BBC Elite' control set.

  • '<'/'>' - Roll left/right
  • 's'/'x' - Climb/dive
  • 'z'/'c' - Yaw left/right
  • 'space' - Thrust
  • '?' - Brake ... (Relative to what???)
  • 'm' - Fire 'Asteroids style' dumb missile. Hold down for auto-fire.
  • 'p' - Pause/unpause game.
