The Universe

The universe is a relativily small (a few km across) box. The ship's location inside it is displayed by the 'position' indicator.

However, like asteroids, the boundaries 'wrap' (only this time it's a 3D cube rather than the 2D screen.)

If evrything is working you, and everything else, should able fly and shoot through 'wrap' without knowing it's there (apart from the position indicator going negative).
