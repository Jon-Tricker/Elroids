Guide to trade.

It is possible to trade minerals and ship components.

Each system has a unique 'technology level','magic level','law level' and 'abundance of minerals'. These effects the value of things when trading

Trading minerals

Minerals are obtaind by breaking up 'rocks' and picking up the 'Minerals' they release.

Minerals can be sold, using the 'Cargo Menu', when docked at a station.

The selling price is dependant on the base price of the mineral and it's abundance in the selling system (rare minerals command higher prices).

It is thus a good idea to mine in a system where a mineral is common and then sell in one where it's scarce.

Trading ship components

Components can be bought, when docked at a station, using the 'Purchase Menu'. They can either be:

  • Bought - Put into the cargo bay for future mounting/sale.
  • Mounted - Installed into the ship (if there is a free slot for the component).

Components can later be sold using the 'Cargo Menu'.

Components can only be bought in systems with a high enough 'Tech Level' to manufacture them.

Component sale values are higher systems that lack the 'Tech Level' necessary to maufacture the component.
