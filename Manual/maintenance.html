Maintenance

The ship is made up of a number of 'components'.

Each component can be either:

  • 'Mounted' - Installed and working on the ship.
  • 'Unounted' - Stored in the cargo bay (displayed in the 'Cargo Menu'.
  • 'Sold' - Sold off for credits.

Component trading

Componets can only be bought/sold or mounted/unmounted when docked at a station.

New componets can be bought via the 'Purchase Menu'. They can ether be bought (into the cargo bay) or mounted directly into an empty component slot.

WARNING!

DONT UNDOCK WITH AN INCOMPLETE SHIP (E.G. NO ENGINE). YOU MAY HAVE NO WAY OF RE-DOCKING!
