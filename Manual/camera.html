Camera

There are a number of camera 'angles'. Historically these were implemented but, at present, 'Pilot' seems to be the most useful and is the default. However others are available. Select with numeric keys.

  • '1' - 'Pilot'. Pilot's eye view.
  • '2' - 'Chase ship'. Following just behind and above ship.
  • '3' - 'Tracking'. Follows ship but does not turn with it.
  • '4' - 'Fixed'. Ship can fly out of shot.
