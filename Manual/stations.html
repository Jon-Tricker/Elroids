Staions

Stations are reprented as the classic '2001 donut' with a central docking bay.

By convention stations are located near to the positional origin (point 0,0,0).

They are immobile and indestructable.

These are where you:

  • Get afordable ship repairs.

Eventually will also be able to:

  • Sell minerals.
  • Buy/sell trade goods.
  • Buy additional ship components.

Docking.

For now is quite simple. Just line up on the bay (marked with a cross) and fly in at less than 10 m/s.

If you hit any other part of the station will collide as normal.

At present docking is quite 'generous' only the centre point of the ship had to be in the bay.

Un-docking

To launch select 'undock' from the menu. The ship will be undocked and positioned just outside the docking bay.
